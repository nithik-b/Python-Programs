Flowers are beautiful parts of plants that often come in various shapes, sizes, and colors.
They grow in gardens, parks, and wild areas, adding charm to our surroundings.
Flowers play an essential role in nature by attracting bees, butterflies, and other pollinators, which help plants reproduce.
Many people enjoy giving and receiving flowers as gifts to express feelings like love, friendship, and sympathy.
Common types of flowers include roses, tulips, and daisies, each with its own meaning and beauty.
Overall, flowers not only brighten our environment but also bring joy and connection among people.
